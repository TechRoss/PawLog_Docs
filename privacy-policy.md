# PawLog Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 2026_

PawLog is a personal pet care app built for private use. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Storage

All data you enter into PawLog (pet profiles, care logs, medications, vet visits, walk history, contacts) is stored **locally on your device only**. It is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

## Location / GPS

PawLog uses your device's GPS during active walk tracking only. Location data is used solely to calculate walk distance and is stored locally on your device. Location is never transmitted externally.

## Weather

PawLog fetches weather data from [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com) using your approximate location. No personal information is sent — only a latitude/longitude coordinate to retrieve local conditions.

## Photos

Pet profile photos are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded or shared.

## Notifications

PawLog may send local notifications for care reminders and medication alerts. These are generated on-device and do not involve any external service.

## Data Deletion

To delete all app data, uninstall the app. You can also use the Backup & Restore screen to wipe all data from within the app.

## Contact

This app is developed and maintained by Ross Bateman. For questions, visit the [PawLog GitHub repository](https://github.com/TechRoss/PawLog_Docs).
