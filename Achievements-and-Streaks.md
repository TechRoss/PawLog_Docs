# Achievements & Streaks

PawLog celebrates consistency and milestones with **streaks** and **achievements**. Both are visible in the **History → Awards** tab.

---

## Streaks Explained

A **streak** is the number of consecutive days you've completed a specific task without missing a day.

- Each task has its own streak
- Completing the task today adds 1 to the streak
- Missing a day (without an excused skip) resets the streak to 0
- **Excused skips** (swipe left on the task in the Routine tab) are transparent — they don't break or extend the streak; it carries over as if that day didn't count

> 📷 *Screenshot: Task card showing a streak count — e.g. "🔥 14 day streak"*

Streaks are shown:
- On each task card in the **Routine** tab
- In the **History → Awards** sub-tab

---

## Achievements

Achievements are one-off badges earned when you hit a milestone. They appear in the **History → Awards** tab with the date they were earned.

| Badge | Name | How to Earn |
|---|---|---|
| 🛁 | **Squeaky Clean** | Log a bath for your pet for the first time |
| 🧼 | **Bath Club** | Log 3 baths for your pet |

More achievements are planned for future versions of PawLog!

> 📷 *Screenshot: Earned achievement card showing the 🛁 Squeaky Clean badge with the date it was earned*

---

## How Achievements Are Triggered

Achievements are checked automatically whenever you complete a relevant task. For bath achievements, PawLog looks for completions of bath-related tasks. You don't need to do anything special — just keep logging your pet's care and the badges will appear when you hit the milestones.
