# Home Screen

The **Home** tab is your daily dashboard — a quick overview of what's going on with your pet right now.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Home screen for Rigby showing the pet card, weather indicator, progress summary, and emergency button*

---

## Layout Overview

- **Pet selector** — at the top, tap to switch between pets (appears when you have 2+ pets)
- **Pet profile card** — shows your pet's name, breed, age, and photo
- **Weather safety indicator** — shows the current "feels like" temperature and a safety note for walking
- **Emergency button** — a prominent button for quick access to emergency features
- **Quick stats** — a snapshot of today's routine progress

---

## Weather Safety Indicator

PawLog uses your device's location and the Open-Meteo weather service to fetch the current "feels like" temperature. It uses this to give you a heads-up about whether conditions are safe for a walk — for example, warning you if it's too hot for paw pads on the pavement, or too cold for smaller breeds.

- No account or API key needed — it works automatically
- Requires location permission on your device for accurate results

> 📷 *Screenshot: Weather card showing a temperature reading and safety message*

## Emergency Button

The large **Emergency** button on the home screen gives you instant one-tap access to:

- Emergency vet contact with direct calling
- Your pet's Emergency Info Card (a quick-reference summary)
- Your saved emergency contacts

This is designed to be fast — if you're in a panic, you don't want to hunt through menus.

See [[Emergency Features]] for full details.
