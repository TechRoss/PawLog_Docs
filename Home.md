# Welcome to PawLog

> *Your friendly companion for keeping on top of your pet's daily care.*

PawLog is a pet care companion app designed to help you stay on top of everything your dog (or any other pet!) needs every single day. Whether it's a morning walk, a flea treatment, a vet check-up, or just making sure they got their evening medication — PawLog keeps it all in one place so nothing slips through the cracks.

## What PawLog Helps You With

- **Daily care routines** — tick off walks, grooming, and any task you set up, day by day
- **Medications** — track what needs to be given and when, with stock level monitoring
- **Vet visits** — keep a full medical history journal for your pet
- **Weight tracking** — log weights over time and see how your pet is doing
- **Walk tracking** — time your walks with GPS and build a history of how long you've been out
- **Streaks & achievements** — stay motivated with streak tracking and fun milestone badges
- **Emergency readiness** — one-tap access to emergency vets, contacts, and a quick-reference info card for your pet

PawLog stores everything **locally on your device** — your pet's data stays private and is always available even without an internet connection.

---

## Getting Around the App

PawLog is organised into five tabs at the bottom of the screen:

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Daily dashboard — pet profile, weather safety, emergency access |
| **Routine** | Daily care task list — tick off and track your pet's care |
| **Health** | Medications, weight tracking, and vet visit journal |
| **History** | Streaks, achievements, and walk history |
| **More** | Settings, notifications, emergency contacts, backup |
