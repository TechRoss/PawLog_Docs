# Managing Your Pets

PawLog supports multiple pets — so if Rigby has a feline sibling, they can have their own profile too.

---

## Adding a New Pet

1. From the **Home** tab, tap the **+** button or **Add Pet**.
2. Fill in the profile form and tap **Save**.

---

## Editing a Pet's Profile

1. On the **Home** tab, tap the pencil ✏️ icon next to the pet's name.
2. Update any fields you like.
3. Tap **Save** to apply the changes.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Pet profile edit screen showing all editable fields*

---

## Adding or Changing a Profile Photo

Inside the pet profile edit screen:

1. Tap the profile photo area (or the camera icon).
2. Choose **Take Photo** to use your camera, or **Choose from Gallery** to pick an existing photo.
3. The photo will appear on their profile card and throughout the app.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Pet profile edit screen showing photo picker options — Take Photo / Choose from Gallery*

---

## Deleting a Pet

> **Warning:** Deleting a pet permanently removes **all** of their data — tasks, care logs, medications, weight records, vet visits, achievements, and streaks. This cannot be undone.

1. Open the pet's profile by tapping the pencil ✏️ icon.
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Pet**.
3. Confirm when prompted.

---

## Switching Between Pets

Whenever there is more than one pet in PawLog, a **pet selector** appears at the top of most screens. It has an **orange border** so it's easy to spot. Tap a pet's name or photo to switch the current view to that pet.
