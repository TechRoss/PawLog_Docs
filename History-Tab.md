# History Tab

The **History** tab is where you look back on all the great work you've been doing with your pet. It has two sub-tabs: **Awards** and **Walks**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: History tab showing the Awards and Walks sub-tab switcher at the top*

---

## Awards Sub-Tab (Default View)

The **Awards** sub-tab is the default view when you open the History tab. It shows:

- **Current streaks** — for every task you have set up, showing the current consecutive day count
- **Achievements** — milestone badges you've earned

> 📷 *Screenshot: Awards sub-tab showing a streak list and earned achievement badges*

See [[Achievements and Streaks]] for details on how streaks work and what achievements are available.

---

## Walks Sub-Tab

The **Walks** sub-tab shows a history of all completed walks, organised by:

- **Date** — walks are grouped by day
- **Duration** — shown in hours, minutes, and seconds (e.g. "42m 15s")
- **Distance** — shown if GPS was active (e.g. "1.2 mi" or "2.0 km"), in your preferred units
- **Task name** — which walk task it was (e.g. "Morning Walk", "Evening Walk")

Only tasks with **Walk Tracking** enabled will appear here. This gives you a satisfying record of how much time you've spent out and about with your pet.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Walks sub-tab showing a list grouped by date, each entry showing the walk task name and duration*
