# Routine Tab

The **Routine** tab is where your daily pet care happens. Think of it as a to-do list for your pet, refreshed every day.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Routine tab showing today's task list for Rigby with a progress bar at the top*

---

## Daily Task List

Each task appears as a card showing:

- The task name (e.g. "Morning Walk", "Teeth Brushing", "Flea Treatment")
- Whether it has been completed today
- The current streak for that task

At the top of the screen there is a **progress bar** showing how many of today's tasks have been completed — e.g. "3 of 5 done".

---

## Completing a Task

Tap the circle/tick button on the task card to mark it as done for today. The card will update immediately and your streak for that task increases by 1.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Task card before and after ticking — showing the completed state with a green tick*

---

## Skipping a Task with a Reason

Sometimes you genuinely can't do a task — Rigby is at the groomer's, you're travelling, or it's just one of those days. PawLog lets you **excuse** a task for the day without breaking your streak.

**How to skip a task:**

1. **Swipe left** on the task card.
2. A list of reasons appears — choose the one that fits:
   - Unwell / not feeling up to it
   - Bad weather
   - Pet with sitter / on holiday
   - Vet's advice
   - No stock / ran out
   - Too busy today
   - Other
3. Tap your chosen reason.

The task card updates to show: **⏭ Skipped: [your reason]**

Your streak is **protected** — an excused skip is treated as a transparent day, not a missed one.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Swipe left action revealed on a task card, showing the reason list*

> 📷 *Screenshot: Task card in the excused state showing "⏭ Skipped: Bad weather"*

## Streaks

Every task has its own streak counter — the number of consecutive days you've completed it without missing.

- Completing a task increases the streak by 1
- Missing a day (without excusing it) resets the streak to 0
- **Excused skips do not reset the streak** — the streak carries over as if that day didn't count

Streaks are shown on each task card and in the **History → Awards** tab.
