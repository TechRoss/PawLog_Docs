# Emergency Features

PawLog has a set of emergency features designed to be accessible quickly when you need them most. They are all reachable with one tap from the home screen.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Home screen highlighting the large Emergency button*

---

## Emergency Button

The **Emergency** button on the **Home** screen is always visible. Tap it to open the emergency menu with access to all emergency features.

---

## Emergency Vet Screen

The Emergency Vet screen gives you **one-tap calling** to your emergency vet.

1. Tap **Emergency** on the home screen.
2. Tap **Emergency Vet**.
3. Your emergency vet's number is shown — tap **Call** to dial immediately.

> Make sure your emergency vet's contact is saved in [[Emergency Contacts]] before you need it.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Emergency Vet screen with a large Call button and the vet's phone number displayed*

---

## Emergency Info Card

The **Emergency Info Card** is a read-only summary of your pet's key details — useful to show a vet, a pet sitter, or anyone looking after your pet in an emergency. It's designed to be quick to pull up and easy to read at a glance.

The card includes:

- Pet name, breed, and date of birth
- Key notes from the profile
- Current medications (name, dose, and frequency)

**Accessing the Emergency Info Card:**

1. Tap **Emergency** on the home screen.
2. Tap **Emergency Info Card**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Emergency Info Card for Rigby showing breed, age notes, and medication list*

---

## Switching Pets on the Info Card

If you have multiple pets, a **pet selector** appears at the top of the Emergency Info Card so you can quickly switch between pets and see the right details. This is handy at the vet if you've brought more than one animal.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Emergency Info Card with the pet selector visible at the top, switching between two pets*
