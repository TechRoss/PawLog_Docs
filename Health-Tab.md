# Health Tab

The **Health** tab is your pet's health hub. From here you can manage medications, track weight, and keep a journal of vet visits.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Health tab showing the "Due Today" medication section and navigation buttons for Medications, Weight, and Vet Visits*

---

## What's on the Health Tab

### Due Today

At the top of the Health tab is a **Due Today** section. This shows any medications that are scheduled for today and haven't been given yet — a quick glance so you never miss a dose without having to navigate into the full medications screen.

> 📷 *Screenshot: "Due Today" section showing one medication due with a "Mark as Given" button*

### Navigation Buttons

- **Medications** — opens the full medication management screen. See [[Medications]].
- **Weight** — opens the weight history and logging screen. See [[Weight Tracking]].
- **Vet Visits** — opens the vet visit journal. See [[Vet Visits]].

---

## Pet Selector

Like other tabs, the Health tab shows a **pet selector** at the top when you have multiple pets. Switch between pets to see the relevant health information for each one.
