# Getting Started

## First Launch

When you open PawLog for the first time, you'll see a clean, empty app — there's no sample data to wade through. The first thing to do is add your pet.

## Adding Your First Pet

1. Tap the **Home** tab at the bottom of the screen.
2. Tap **Add Pet** (or the **+** button) to open the pet profile form.
3. Fill in your pet's details:
   - **Name** — e.g. Rigby
   - **Breed** — e.g. Cavapoo
   - **Date of Birth** — tap the date field to pick from a calendar
   - **Weight** — enter their current weight (you can choose kg or lbs)
   - **Notes** — anything useful, like "scared of thunder" or "allergic to chicken"
4. Optionally add a **profile photo** (see [[Managing Your Pets]] for details).
5. Tap **Save**.

That's it — your pet is in the app! You can now start building out their daily routine and health records.

## What to Set Up Next

Once your first pet is saved, we recommend doing these things to get the most out of PawLog:

- **Add daily tasks** on the **Routine** tab (walks, brushing, grooming, etc.) — see [[Managing Tasks]]
- **Add any medications** on the **Health** tab — see [[Medications]]
- **Set a daily reminder notification** in **More → Notification Settings** — see [[Notifications]]
- **Add emergency contacts** in **More → Emergency Contacts** — see [[Emergency Contacts]]

---

## Adding More Pets

PawLog supports multiple pets. To add a second pet, follow the same steps above — tap **Add Pet** from the Home tab any time. See [[Managing Your Pets]] for more.
