# Vet Visits

PawLog includes a full **vet visit journal** so you can keep a complete medical history for your pet in one place — useful for spotting patterns, sharing with new vets, or just having everything handy at appointments.

---

## Getting to Vet Visits

From the **Health** tab, tap **Vet Visits**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Vet Visits list showing visit cards with visit type badge, date, clinic name, and follow-up chip*

---

## Recording a Vet Visit

1. Tap **+ Add Visit**.
2. Fill in the visit details:
   - **Date and Time** — defaults to now; adjust as needed
   - **Visit Type** — choose from:
     - Routine Check-up
     - Vaccination
     - Illness
     - Surgery
     - Dental
     - Emergency
     - Other
   - **Reason for Visit** — a brief description (e.g. "Annual check-up and boosters")
   - **Clinic Name** — the vet practice name
   - **Vet Name** — the vet who saw your pet
   - **Clinical Notes** — what the vet found, any diagnoses or observations
   - **Care Plan** — follow-up instructions, treatments prescribed, dietary changes, etc.
3. Optionally toggle on **Has Follow-up** and set a follow-up date.
4. Optionally enable a **Follow-up Reminder** — PawLog will notify you when the follow-up date arrives.
5. Tap **Save**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Add Vet Visit form showing all fields including clinical notes text area and the follow-up date toggle*

---

## Visit Detail View

Tapping a visit in the list opens the full detail view, showing everything you recorded. You may also see **reminder chips** — dismissable prompts that appear when a visit suggests you might want to sync information with your medication or vaccination records.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Vet Visit detail view showing all recorded information and any reminder chips*

---

## Editing a Visit

From the visit detail view, tap the pencil ✏️ icon to open the edit form. Make your changes and tap **Save**.
