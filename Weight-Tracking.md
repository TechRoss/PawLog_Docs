# Weight Tracking

Keeping an eye on your pet's weight is an important part of their long-term health. PawLog makes it easy to log weights over time and see trends at a glance.

---

## Logging a Weight

1. From the **Health** tab, tap **Weight**.
2. Tap **+ Add Weight**.
3. Enter the weight value.
4. Choose **kg** or **lbs** using the unit toggle.
5. The date defaults to today — you can adjust it if you're logging a past measurement.
6. Tap **Save**.

---

## Weight History

The weight history screen shows all your logged weight entries in a chart and list. You can:

- See the trend over time at a glance
- Toggle between **kg** and **lbs** — the display converts automatically
- Scroll through all historical entries
