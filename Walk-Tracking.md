# Walk Tracking

PawLog can time your walks and log them to a history, building a satisfying record of how often and how long you've been out with your pet.

---

## Enabling Walk Tracking on a Task

Walk tracking is opt-in per task. To enable it:

1. Go to **Routine → Manage Tasks** (the cog icon).
2. Tap the pencil ✏️ icon next to your walk task (e.g. "Morning Walk").
3. Toggle on **Enable Walk Tracking**.
4. Tap **Save**.

---

## Starting a Walk

1. On the **Routine** tab, tap the walk task card (any task with walk tracking enabled).
2. The **Walk Session** screen opens.
3. If you have multiple pets with walk-tracking tasks, a **"Who's coming?"** selector appears — tick the pets joining this walk.
4. Tap **Start Walk**.

The timer begins immediately.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Walk Session screen just started — showing the live timer, GPS indicator, and "Who's coming?" pet selector*

---

## Live Walk Screen

While a walk is in progress you can see:

- **Live timer** — hours, minutes, and seconds elapsed
- **Distance** — updates as you walk, shown in miles or kilometres depending on your unit preference
- **GPS tracking** — your location is tracked throughout the walk, even when the screen is off (requires location permission)

A **"Walk in progress 🐾"** notification appears in your status bar while tracking. This keeps the GPS running in the background so the distance is accurate even if you lock your phone or switch to another app.

The walk is saved when you tap **Start Walk**, so if you navigate away and come back, the timer is still running correctly.

---

## Ending a Walk

1. When you're back home, tap the walk task on the **Routine** tab to return to the Walk Session screen.
2. Tap **End Walk**.
3. The walk is saved with the total duration, and:
   - The task is marked as completed for today
   - The streak updates for all pets that came along
   - The walk appears in **History → Walks**

> 📷 *Screenshot: Walk Session screen showing elapsed time with the End Walk button ready to tap*

## Multi-Pet Walks

If you have two or more pets with walk-tracking tasks enabled, the **"Who's coming?"** selector appears on the Walk Session screen.

- Check each pet that is joining the walk
- Your selection is saved as soon as the walk starts — you can navigate away and come back without losing it
- When the walk ends, **all selected pets** get their task logged and their streak updated
- Each pet's walk appears correctly in their own walk history

> 📷 *Screenshot: "Who's coming?" pet selector showing Rigby checked and a second pet available to select*

---

## GPS Note

Walk tracking uses your device's GPS. For best results:

- Grant PawLog location permission when prompted
- Make sure location services are turned on in your phone's settings
- GPS accuracy improves once you're outdoors and away from buildings
- The persistent notification in your status bar confirms GPS is running — do not dismiss it mid-walk or tracking will stop
