# Holiday Mode

Going away and leaving Rigby with a sitter? Holiday Mode tells PawLog to stand down while you're away, so you don't come back to broken streaks and a backlog of missed notifications.

---

## Setting a Holiday Date Range

1. Go to **More → Notification Settings**.
2. Scroll to the **Holiday Mode** section.
3. Toggle on **Holiday Mode**.
4. Set the **Start Date** and **End Date** of your holiday.
5. Tap **Save**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Notification Settings screen showing the Holiday Mode section with start and end date pickers*

---

## What Holiday Mode Does

While your holiday date range is active, PawLog:

- **Back-fills excused logs** — when you open the app during or after your holiday, all tasks for all pets are automatically excused for every day of the holiday period. This means no streaks are broken.
- **Suppresses notifications** — daily reminders and medication alerts are not sent during the holiday period, so you won't be disturbed while you're away.
- **Sends a "Welcome back" notification** — on the end date of your holiday, PawLog sends a friendly reminder that the holiday is over and normal care logging resumes.

---

## After Your Holiday

When you return, simply open PawLog. The app will back-fill the excused logs automatically. Your streaks will be intact, and you're ready to pick up right where you left off.

---

## Tip

Set up Holiday Mode **before** you leave, not when you get back. That way the notification suppression kicks in from day one of your trip.
