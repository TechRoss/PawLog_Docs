# Medications

PawLog's medication tracking helps you manage everything from daily flea prevention to split-dose antibiotics — making sure every dose is given on time and stock is monitored.

---

## Adding a Medication

1. From the **Health** tab, tap **Medications**.
2. Tap **+ Add Medication**.
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name** — e.g. "Simparica Trio", "Apoquel", "Amoxicillin"
   - **Frequency** — how often it is given (e.g. Daily, Monthly)
   - **Stock** — how many doses/tablets you currently have
   - **Dose Times** — the time(s) of day each dose is given
4. Tap **Save**.

## Split-Dose Medications

Some medications need to be given more than once a day — for example, an antibiotic given morning and evening. PawLog handles this with **multiple dose times**.

When adding or editing a medication:

1. Tap **Add Dose Time** to add another time slot.
2. Set each time independently (e.g. 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM).
3. Each dose time is tracked separately — both need to be marked as given for the day to be fully covered.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Medication detail showing two dose times — 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM — each with their own "Mark as Given" button*

---

## Marking a Dose as Given

From the **Health** tab's "Due Today" section or from the **Medications** screen:

1. Find the medication and the specific dose time.
2. Tap **Mark as Given** (or the tick button).
3. The dose is logged with the current time.

---

## Stock Tracking

PawLog automatically decrements the stock count **once per day** when a medication is marked as given. If a medication has multiple dose times, the stock only goes down once regardless of how many times it is marked that day.

- When stock is getting low, the medication card shows a warning
- Update the stock level manually any time by editing the medication (e.g. after picking up a refill)

> 📷 *Screenshot: Medication card showing a low stock warning with the current count displayed*

---

## Editing or Deleting a Medication

1. From the **Medications** screen, tap the pencil ✏️ icon on the medication card.
2. Make your changes and tap **Save**, or scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Medication** to remove it.

---

## Medication History

The **Medication History** screen gives you a full log of every dose that has been given, going back 90 days.

To open it: go to the **Health** tab and tap **Medication History 📋**.

The log is grouped by date and shows:

- The medication name
- The dose time (e.g. "Morning", "Evening") where applicable
- The exact time the dose was recorded

Entries are written automatically whenever you mark a dose as given, and removed if you untick it. If you edit the time a dose was given, the history entry updates to match.
