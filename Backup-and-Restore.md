# Backup & Restore

PawLog stores everything on your device, which is great for privacy — but it means you should back up your data regularly, especially before switching phones or clearing app data.

---

## Creating a Backup

1. Go to the **More** tab.
2. Tap **Backup & Restore**.
3. Tap **Export Backup**.
4. PawLog creates a JSON file containing all your data and saves it to your device's **Downloads** folder.
5. A confirmation message appears when the export is complete.

---

## What's Included in a Backup

The backup file contains everything PawLog knows about your pets:

- Pet profiles (name, breed, birthday, notes, photo)
- All care tasks and their settings
- Care logs (completed and excused)
- Medications, dose times, and dose logs
- Medication history (every dose given)
- Weight records
- Vet visit records
- Vaccinations
- Emergency contacts
- Achievements and streaks
- Walk history (duration and distance)

---

## Restoring from a Backup

> **Warning:** Restoring a backup **replaces all current data** in the app. If you have data in PawLog that isn't in the backup file, it will be lost. Export a fresh backup first if you want to preserve your current state.

1. Go to **More → Backup & Restore**.
2. Tap **Restore from File**.
3. Your device's file picker opens — navigate to your backup file (a `.json` file).
4. Tap the file to select it.
5. Confirm the restore when prompted.
6. PawLog resets and imports all data from the backup file.

---

## Keeping Your Backup Safe

- After exporting, **copy the file from Downloads to cloud storage** (Google Drive, OneDrive, etc.) so it's safe even if your phone is lost, reset, or damaged
- Export a fresh backup **before clearing app data** or factory resetting your phone
- Export **before and after major updates**, just in case
- A good habit: export a backup after any big changes — adding a new pet, recording a vet visit, or updating medications
