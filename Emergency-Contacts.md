# Emergency Contacts

PawLog lets you save important contacts — your vet, a pet sitter, a trusted friend — and call or navigate to them directly from within the app.

---

## Getting to Emergency Contacts

From the **More** tab, tap **Emergency Contacts**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Emergency Contacts list showing contact cards with name, phone number, and Call/Map action buttons*

---

## Adding a Contact

1. Tap **+ Add Contact**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** — e.g. "City Vet Clinic" or "Sarah (pet sitter)"
   - **Phone Number** — the number to call
   - **Address** — for map navigation (optional but recommended for vets)
   - **Notes** — any useful info (e.g. "24-hour emergency line", "Only available evenings")
3. Tap **Save**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Add Contact form with name, phone, address, and notes fields*

---

## Calling a Contact

From the contacts list, tap the **Call** (phone icon) button on a contact card. PawLog will open your device's phone dialler with the number pre-filled.

---

## Navigating to a Contact's Address

If an address is saved, tap the **Map** (location pin icon) button on the contact card. Your device's maps app will open with directions to that address — useful when rushing to an emergency vet clinic you don't usually visit.

---

## Editing or Deleting a Contact

Tap the pencil ✏️ icon on a contact card to open the edit form. Make your changes and tap **Save**, or scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Contact** to remove it.

---

## Tip

Add at least these three contacts for the best emergency coverage:

- Your **regular vet** (daytime number)
- Your nearest **24-hour emergency animal hospital**
- A trusted **friend or family member** who can help in a crisis
