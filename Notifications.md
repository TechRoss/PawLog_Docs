# Notifications

PawLog can send you daily reminders and medication alerts so you never forget your pet's care routine, even on busy days.

---

## Setting Up Notifications

1. Go to the **More** tab.
2. Tap **Notification Settings**.
3. Configure your preferences and tap **Save**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Notification Settings screen showing the daily reminder toggle, time picker, and medication alert time*

---

## Daily Reminder

Enable the **Daily Reminder** toggle and set a time. PawLog will send you a notification at that time each day — a gentle nudge to check your pet's routine.

**Tip:** Set this for a time when you're usually home and have a moment to check on your pet — not first thing in the morning when you're rushing, but maybe late afternoon.

---

## Medication Alerts

You can set a notification time for medication reminders. When enabled, PawLog will alert you when a medication dose is due.

---

## How Notifications Work

- Notifications repeat **daily** on their set schedule — once set up, they keep going automatically
- Notifications survive app restarts and phone reboots
- Notifications are **suppressed during Holiday Mode** — see [[Holiday Mode]]

---

## Android Permission Note

On **Android 12 and later**, two permissions are required for notifications to work correctly:

### 1. Notification Permission
Android will prompt you to allow notifications the first time you set them up. Tap **Allow** when asked.

### 2. Exact Alarm Permission
This one needs to be granted manually:

1. Go to your phone's **Settings**
2. Search for **Alarms & Reminders** (or navigate to Apps → PawLog → Permissions)
3. Enable **Allow setting alarms and reminders** for PawLog

> 📷 *Screenshot: Android Alarms & Reminders permission screen in phone Settings with PawLog listed*

Without the exact alarm permission, scheduled notifications may not fire at the precise time you've set.
