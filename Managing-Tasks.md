# Managing Tasks

You build your own task list in PawLog — there are no pre-loaded tasks. This means your routine is completely tailored to your pet.

---

## Getting to Task Management

From the **Routine** tab, tap the **Manage Tasks** button (or the cog/settings icon at the top of the screen).

> 📷 *Screenshot: Task Management screen showing a list of tasks with edit and delete controls*

---

## Adding a Task

1. Tap the **+** button or **Add Task**.
2. Fill in the task details:
   - **Name** — what the task is (e.g. "Evening Walk", "Heartworm Tablet", "Bath")
   - **Frequency** — how often this task applies:
     - **Daily** — shows up every single day
     - **Weekly** — pick a specific day of the week (e.g. every Monday)
     - **Monthly** — pick a specific date each month (e.g. the 1st)
   - **Enable Walk Tracking** — toggle this on if this is a walk task (see below and [[Walk Tracking]])
3. Tap **Save**.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Add Task / Task Detail form showing name, frequency picker, and walk tracking toggle*

---

## Editing a Task

1. On the Task Management screen, tap the pencil ✏️ icon next to the task.
2. Update any fields.
3. Tap **Save**.

---

## Deleting a Task

1. On the Task Management screen, tap the **delete** (trash) icon next to the task.
2. Confirm the deletion.

> **Note:** Deleting a task removes all its historical care logs and streak data.

---

## Reordering Tasks

Press and hold a task card, then drag it up or down to change the order. The order you set here is how tasks appear in your daily **Routine** list.

> 📷 *Screenshot: Task Management screen showing a task being dragged to reorder*

---

## Walk Tracking Toggle

On the task detail form, there is an **Enable Walk Tracking** toggle. Turn this on for any task that represents a walk.

When walk tracking is enabled on a task:

- Tapping that task on the Routine tab opens the **Walk Session** screen with a live timer and GPS tracking
- Completed walks are recorded with their duration
- Walk history appears in **History → Walks**

See [[Walk Tracking]] for full details on how walk sessions work.
